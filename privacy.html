Privacy Policy

Dog Translator: Games for dog

Last Updated: June 15, 2025

1. Introduction

Thank you for using [App Name] ("the App," "we," "us"). Your privacy is very important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use and protect it when you use our application.

2. Important Notice: "For Entertainment Purposes" Principle

Our "Human to Dog Translator" and "Dog to Human Translator" features are not scientifically accurate and operate strictly as a simulation for entertainment purposes.

  • Translation Simulation: The app does not perform a real translation by analyzing your voice or your dog's voice. Instead, it simulates the process and provides predefined, random sounds or text.
  • Data Usage: Microphone access is requested for these features, but your voice data is not analyzed, stored, or sent to our servers in any way.

3. Information We Collect

We collect and process certain information to provide the features of our app. The vast majority of this information is stored on your device and is not sent to our servers.

a. Information You Provide to Us

  • Dog Profile Information: Your dog's name, breed, age, and photo. This information is used to personalize your app experience and is stored only on your device.
  • Character and Health Information: Information you may enter, such as your dog's likes/dislikes, personality traits, health logs, or reminders. This data is also entirely yours and is stored only on your device.
  • Chat Messages: When you use the "AI Chat" feature, the messages you type are sent to our third-party AI service provider to generate a response.

b. Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Metrics: Data such as your total number of translations and chat messages are stored on your device (`@AppStorage`) to track your free usage limits.
  • Device Permissions:
    • Microphone Access: Required to initiate the translation simulations.
    • Photo Library Access: Required for you to select a profile picture for your dog.
    • Notification Permission: Required for us to send you reminders you set up.

c. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, personal email address (unless you contact us for support), precise location, or your contacts.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide the core functionality of the app and personalize your experience.
  • To manage free usage rights and enable Premium subscription features.
  • To provide you with responses in the AI Chat feature.
  • To send in-app reminders and notifications.
  • To respond to your support requests.

5. Data Processing and Third-Party Services

The security and transparency of your data are our priority. Our app utilizes both on-device processing and trusted third-party services to deliver specific features.

Feature Data Processing Location Description and Service Used
Translation Simulations, Profile, Health Logs, Reminders On-Device All your personal profile data, voice recordings, and other inputs are processed and stored solely on your own device. This data is not sent to our servers.
AI Chat Assistant Third-Party Server Your chat messages are processed by Segmind (or a similar AI service) to generate responses. You can review the Segmind Privacy Policy.
Subscriptions and Purchases Third-Party Server We use RevenueCat to verify and manage your subscription status. Your payments are processed directly by the Apple App Store. You can review the RevenueCat Privacy Policy.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable technical and administrative measures to secure your data. The fact that the majority of your information is stored only on your device is a key element that enhances security. However, please be aware that no system is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
